Is a Social Work Career Right for You?

This career is a calling that demands empathy, resilience, and a passion for justice. Explore the traits of successful social workers and weigh the profound rewards against the significant challenges.

Key Personality Traits

Successful social workers often exhibit a unique blend of compassion and conscientiousness. They are driven to help others but also organized and resilient enough to navigate complex systems.

  • ✓
    High Agreeableness: Kindness, compassion, and a strong ability to build trust.
  • ✓
    High Conscientiousness: Organized, diligent, and reliable in managing heavy caseloads and documentation.
  • ✓
    Resilience: The ability to manage stress and bounce back from emotionally demanding situations.
  • ✓
    Strong Communication: Includes active listening to make clients feel heard and understood.

Ethical Foundation: The NASW Code

The profession is guided by six core values that shape every aspect of the work:

1. Service

2. Social Justice

3. Dignity of the Person

4. Importance of Human Relationships

5. Integrity

6. Competence
